Three Sacred Paths

Discover the ancient wisdom of Shiva Swarodaya through modern practice. Choose the path that matches your current state and energy needs.

Idā

Ida Nadi

Left • Cooling

Cool, calming energy. Perfect for study, creativity, and evening relaxation. Activates the parasympathetic nervous system for deep focus.

Piṅ

Pingala Nadi

Right • Heating

Warm, energizing flow. Ideal for morning activation, exercise, and active tasks. Stimulates the sympathetic nervous system for alertness.

Su

Sushumna Nadi

Both • Balanced

Balanced, centered energy. Supports meditation and transitions. Best for resets between activities and deep spiritual practice.
