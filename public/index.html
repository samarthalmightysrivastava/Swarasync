Three Sacred Paths

Discover the ancient wisdom of Shiva Swarodaya through modern practice. Choose the path that matches your current state and energy needs.

Idā

Left • Cooling

Cooling, reflective energy. Perfect for calm focus, study, creating, and winding down. Ideal for evening practice and deep work.

Piṅgala

Right • Heating

Bright, active energy. Supports action, speaking, workouts, and decisive tasks. Perfect for morning practice and energizing activities.

Suṣumnā

Both • Balanced

Balanced, centered energy. Supports meditation and transitions. Best for resets between activities and deep spiritual practice.
