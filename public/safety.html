Important information about using Swarasync safely

⚠️ Important Safety Notice

Swarasync is a calming rhythm game for breathing practice — it is not medical advice and is not a medical device.

Safety Guidelines

  • No breath holds: This app guides natural, comfortable breathing only. Never hold your breath or strain.
  • Stop immediately if you experience dizziness, shortness of breath, chest pain, or any discomfort.
  • Medical conditions: Consult your physician before use if you have heart conditions, lung conditions, are pregnant, or have any medical concerns.
  • Age guidance: Recommended for ages 16+ or with guardian consent and supervision.
  • Listen to your body: Never force your breathing pattern or push beyond what feels natural and comfortable.
  • Environment: Practice in a safe, comfortable environment where you can sit or stand securely.

Acceptable Use: Use this app responsibly and in accordance with these guidelines. Do not use it while driving, operating machinery, or in any situation requiring your full attention.

Content: All breathing techniques are based on traditional practices. Consult qualified teachers for deeper study.

Privacy

  • Local storage only: By default, your practice data stays on your device.
  • No tracking: We do not track your usage or collect personal information.
  • No servers: This is a client-side app that works offline.
  • Future features: Any cloud features will be opt-in with clear disclosure.
